Help Center

How do I change my profile information?
You can change your target weight and profile information in the profile page of the app by clicking on the gear icon button that is in the upper right corner.
How does the app calculate my goals?
We use your inputted age, gender, current weight, and target weight to calculate the total amount of calories you should be eating in a day.
How do I add food to my food diary?
On the home page, tap the "+" button that is located to the left of the whatever meal of the day you would like to add the food to. It will take you to the add food page and you can search for the food you can add.
How do I track my weight progress?
On the progress page there is a graph of your weight progress. Whenever you would like to update your weight progress just enter your new weights in the add weight box and hit the submit button.
What is the nutrition page for?
The nutrition page keeps track of your macronutrient breakdown of all the foods you have eaten for that day. It will show what percentages of the foods that you have eaten are of protein, fat and carbohydrates.
